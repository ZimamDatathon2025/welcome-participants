# Welcome to the ZIMAM AI Datathon 2025

Welcome to the ZIMAM AI Datathon 2025, an intensive two-and-a-half-day event focused on generating innovative, AI-driven solutions from real-world healthcare datasets. This datathon is part of the 8th ZIMAM Digital Health Forum and will take place **November 6-8, 2025** in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

## Event Overview

This intensive datathon focuses on generating innovative, AI-driven solutions from real-world healthcare datasets, specifically **eICU** and **MIMIC-IV**. The event facilitates crucial interdisciplinary dialogue and frameworks for building trustworthy, transparent, and impactful AI in healthcare.

## Datasets

Participants will work with two major critical care datasets:

### MIMIC-IV (Medical Information Mart for Intensive Care)
- Real-world clinical data from Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center
- Comprehensive ICU patient information including vital signs, medications, lab results, and clinical notes
- De-identified patient data for privacy protection

### eICU Collaborative Research Database
- Multi-center ICU database covering diverse geographic regions
- Standardized data from hundreds of ICUs across the United States
- Rich clinical information for comparative effectiveness research

## Team Structure

Participants will be grouped into **multidisciplinary teams** that include:
- Healthcare professionals (physicians, pharmacists, nurses)
- Data scientists and AI researchers
- Clinical researchers and academic faculty
- Domain experts in healthcare AI
- Students and trainees in healthcare or data science

Each team will receive **expert mentorship** from clinical experts and data scientists throughout the event.

## Learning Objectives

By participating in this datathon, you will:

1. **Apply AI Techniques**: Use data science and AI methods to address real-world clinical challenges with healthcare datasets
2. **Collaborate Effectively**: Work within multidisciplinary teams to formulate research questions and develop evidence-based solutions
3. **Manage Healthcare Data**: Gain confidence in handling diverse real-world healthcare datasets and addressing data integrity challenges
4. **Address AI Challenges**: Identify and discuss key challenges in healthcare AI, including algorithmic bias, regulatory constraints, and ethical considerations
5. **Develop Equitable AI**: Understand the importance of aligning AI solutions with diverse patient needs and healthcare priorities
6. **Present Results**: Effectively communicate analytical findings through presentations and reports

## Focus Areas

Teams will analyze real-world data to address clinical questions and develop AI solutions for various aspects of patient care, including:

- Disease prediction and risk stratification
- Diagnosis support and decision-making
- Treatment optimization and personalized medicine
- Clinical outcome prediction
- Resource allocation and operational efficiency

The event also includes discussions on important challenges surrounding AI in healthcare, such as:
- Data bias and fairness
- Ethical considerations in AI deployment
- Regulatory compliance and validation
- Transparency and explainability in AI models
- Equity in healthcare AI applications
